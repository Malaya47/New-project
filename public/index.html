Services & preparation

Operationally clear and customer-friendly.

The service scope and prep rules are surfaced up front so the business stays predictable and premium.

Washing

Standard laundry bag service for everyday garments and regular weekly loads.

Drying

Managed as part of the service flow before items are folded and prepared for return.

Ironing

Folding

All standard loads are folded and returned neatly within the promised turnaround window.

Prepare your laundry

  • Separate white, dark, and colored items.
  • Normal everyday laundry only.
  • Do not overfill the bag.

Important to know

  • Laundry is washed as handed over.
  • No re-sorting of individual garments.
  • Mixed bags are washed together.

Not accepted

  • Silk, wool, and leather.
  • Curtains, blankets, or bulky bedding.
  • Very dirty or specialty-care garments.
